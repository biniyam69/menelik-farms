Rooted in Tradition, Growing for the Future

Nestled in the vibrant and fertile lands of Ethiopia's Gambela region, Menelik and His Family Farming Co is built on a rich heritage of agriculture. We harness the unique climate and nutrient-rich soil to produce crops and goods of unparalleled quality. We blend time-honored farming techniques, passed down through generations, with modern, sustainable practices to cultivate exceptional products.

Our mission is to empower local communities by partnering with small-scale farmers while also investing in significant commercial farming operations, including expansive palm tree plantations. This dual approach ensures high-quality yields and supports the economic growth of the Gambela region, creating a sustainable future for all.

We are dedicated to quality, sustainability, and delivering the authentic taste of Gambela to our global partners.

Our Premium Gambela Exports

From staple grains to specialty goods, our products are a testament to Gambela's rich soil and ideal climate.

Hearty maize, versatile sorghum, and nutritious sesame seeds, cultivated for peak flavor and quality.

Artisanal Goods

Livestock

Palm Cultivation

Connecting Gambela to the World

We pride ourselves on our robust and reliable supply chain, ensuring our agricultural products maintain their freshness and quality from our farms in Gambela, Ethiopia to markets across the globe.

Our key export hub in Minnesota, USA, serves as a vital link to our North American partners. We manage all logistics to provide a seamless and dependable experience, making the finest Gambelan goods accessible to you.
